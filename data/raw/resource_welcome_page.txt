## About the Program

The AI Engineering Bootcamp is an elite training program designed for those seeking to excel in the best practices of building and shipping enterprise-grade AI-powered software. In this bootcamp, participants master advanced AI engineering skills, from building core LLM-powered applications to deploying RAG and agentic systems to production.

The program is led by Ed Donner and Zion Pibowei, and supported by a team of training assistants (TAs). It is a 40-hour-per-week commitment over 10 weeks. The program equips participants with top-tier AI engineering skills that are in high demand across the world's top organisations.

## What Is Expected of Participants

- Show up to sessions. If you are unable to attend, inform your TA in advance.
- Post your daily standup update. Your squad depends on consistent communication.
- Ask for help when you are stuck. Struggling in silence slows progress.
- Support others when you can. Teaching reinforces learning.
- Commit to the work. This is a 40-hour-per-week program for 10 weeks — plan accordingly.
