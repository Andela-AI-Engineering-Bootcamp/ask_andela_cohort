# AI Bootcamp: Protocol for Daily Async Squad Updates

This document outlines the protocol all bootcamp members and squad leads must follow for daily async updates in their squad channels.

---

## Member's Role — Daily Update

All members, including squad leads, are required to provide a daily update by **1:30 PM GMT**.

Use the following format for your update:

- **Course:** State the week and day of the course you have completed. Example: Week 1 Day 5
- **Blockers:** List any technical challenges you need help with.
- **Pull Requests (PRs):** Share the link to any expected pull requests, if applicable.

---

## Lead's Role

Squad leads have two responsibilities each day: creating the daily thread and posting the squad summary.

### 1. Daily Thread Creation

- **Naming Convention:** Squad icon + Week [x] Day [y]. Example: Week 1 Day 2
- **Time Due:** At least 2 hours before 1:45 PM GMT (i.e., by 11:45 AM GMT at the latest)
- Note: The program coordinator will create the threads for the first day; squad leads take over from Wednesday onwards.

### 2. Squad Lead Daily Summary

- **Time Due:** 1:45 PM GMT

Use the following template for the summary post:

**[Squad number]**

**Summary**
- **Stats:** [updates provided] / [total in squad]. Example: 6/6
- If the updates provided do not match the total, provide a breakdown:
  - **Updates From:** [list of members who submitted]
  - **No Updates From:** [list of members who did not submit]
- **Blockers:**
  - [Member name]: one-line description of their issue
