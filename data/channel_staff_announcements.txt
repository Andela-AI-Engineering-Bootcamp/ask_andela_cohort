## Week 2 Overview — AI Engineer Core Track — Maureen Milgo (Program Staff)

Week 2 takes us deeper into applied AI engineering.

This week covers:
- Advanced RAG systems using vector embeddings
- Moving from traditional ML to deep learning
- Fine-tuning frontier models

## Week 3 Overview — AI Leader: Generative AI and Agentic AI for Leaders and Founders — Maureen Milgo

This week is about thinking like an AI leader.

Topics covered this week:
- Fundamentals behind modern AI systems: LLMs, transformers, and generative AI
- How leaders evaluate frontier vs open-source models
- How organisations are strategically implementing generative AI
- Executive AI decision-making: RAG vs fine-tuning, AI agents vs workflows, cross-functional AI strategy frameworks
- Identifying high-value AI projects, building AI teams, planning AI roadmaps, and delivering real ROI
- Two expert sessions with industry practitioners

What is expected from participants this week:
- Stay on track with the lessons
- Join the expert sessions
- Engage in discussions
- Start identifying AI opportunities in your organisation

The goal for the week: move from understanding AI to leading AI. For the detailed weekly breakdown, see: https://community.andela.com/t/program-expectations/163650
