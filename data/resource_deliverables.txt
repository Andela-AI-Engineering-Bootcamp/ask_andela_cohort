# Program Deliverables and Weekly Schedule
Source: Program Staff, A3: AI Engineering Bootcamp resource page

This document describes how participants are assessed throughout the bootcamp and what the weekly schedule looks like.

---

## Deliverables and Assessments

Your performance is assessed through the following deliverables:

| Deliverable | When | How It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Group Projects | Weeks 3, 6, 9 | Collaborative work with your squad, peer reviewed |
| Peer Review | Throughout | You review others' work, they review yours. Pass/fail. |
| Capstone | Weeks 9–10 | Individual project demonstrating what you've learned |
| Behavioral Assignments | Due every Wednesday | Complete an assigned simulation in SolidRoad and receive feedback on how to improve |

Peer review is not graded numerically. It is pass/fail: did you engage seriously with the work? The point is learning from each other, not competing.

---

## Weekly Schedule

The weekly rituals ensure that participants keep pace and that leads can provide support along the way.

| Day | What Happens |
|---|---|
| Monday | Squad standup, TA check-in, Monday kickoff |
| Tuesday | Squad standup, TA check-in, Deep dive (90 min) |
| Wednesday | Squad standup, TA check-in, Behavioral assignments due, Office hours |
| Thursday | Squad standup, TA check-in, Deep dive (90 min) |
| Friday | Squad standup, TA check-in, Office hours, Friday wins |

Deep dives are the main teaching sessions. Office hours are for questions and help. The rest of your time is self-directed: Udemy courses, projects, and peer collaboration.
