# Bootcamp Learning Toolkit
Source: Program Staff, posted on the A3: AI Engineering Bootcamp resource page

Here you'll find all the resources and information you'll need throughout your learning journey. To get started, please use the resources below.

---

## 1. Main Learning Resources (Mandatory)

The core learning content is organised into sequential courses that follow the weekly program schedule. Access to each course will be granted as you progress through the bootcamp.

- Course 1: AI Engineer Core Track — LLM Engineering (start here): https://www.udemy.com/course/llm-engineering-master-ai-and-large-language-models/
- Course 2: AI Leader — Generative AI and Agentic AI for Leaders
- Course 3: AI Engineer Agentic Track
- Course 4: AI Engineer Production Track

---

## 2. Tools and Platforms

To support your learning and collaboration, you'll have access to the following tools throughout the bootcamp:

- **The Andela Community**: For all community interactions and peer discussions (Discourse).
- **Google Meet**: Used for all live sessions, including daily check-ins, deep dives, and office hours.
- **Coding Assistants**: Access to Cursor and GitHub Copilot to accelerate your learning and hands-on practice.
- **SolidRoad** (https://www.solidroad.com): A platform to help you enhance your behavioral and soft skills through AI-powered simulations.
